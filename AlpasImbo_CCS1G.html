INTERNATIONAL STATE COLLEGE OF THE PHILIPPINES


International State College of the Philippines is a prestigious private school nestled in the Philippines.
Established in 2022, International State College of the Philippines has a rich history of academic excellence,
character development, and community engagement. Our school is dedicated to providing a well-rounded
education that prepares students for success in both their personal and professional lives.
