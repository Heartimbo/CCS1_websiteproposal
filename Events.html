ISCP EVENTS

"Welcome to the most spectacularly events of the school. At ISCP, we believe that normal events are overrated, and it's time to celebrate the quirks and talents that make absolutely no sense."

CAFETERIA FOOD SCULPTURE CONTEST

Challenge your creativity by turning school cafeteria food into edible art.
Mold mashed potatoes into masterpieces and fashion spaghetti into sculptures.
Winner gets to eat their creation.

CHALKBOARD ARTISTIC OLYMPICS CONTEST

Express your inner rebel and compete in the artistic vandalism contest.
Students will have 30 minutes to draw the most creative graffiti on the
chalkboards without getting caught.

HALLWAY HIDE AND SEEK

Students will hide throughout the school,
and teachers must seek them out to win small prizes.
Beware of the detention room, though â€“ it's the final frontier!

HOMEWORK BONFIRE BONANZA

Gather around a bonfire and throw your homework assignments into the flames.
It's a symbolic event to celebrate the temporary liberation from the tyranny of homework.
S'mores provided.

INTERGALACTIC INTRAMURALS

Bored and focused only on academics?
Why not challenge the extraterrestrial creatures with sports!
Winner gets a trophy and a trip to other galaxies!

INVISIBLE PET PARADE

Show off your nonexistent pets in the school's Invisible Pet Parade.
Let your imagination run wild as you "walk" your invisible pets around the school grounds.
Bonus points for the most convincing leash-walking.

LUNCH FOOD FIGHT

Tired of the boredom of eating your meals with your friends?
How about initiating a food fight in the cafeteria!
Watch out for the janitors though!

MATHLETE VS. ATHLETE SHOWDOWN

It's a battle of wits and brawn as our mathletes take on the star athletes
in a series of epic challenges, including solving algebra problems while sprinting and
dribbling basketballs during calculus.

NAP TIME OLYMPICS

Students will compete in various napping disciplines,
including synchronized snoozing, the 100-meter power nap, and the snore-relay.
Medals awarded for the most creative sleeping positions.

TEACHER VS. STUDENT DANCE-OFF

Watch the battle of the century as our teacher and student
break out their dance moves to prove who's the real king or queen of the school.
Warning: May involve questionable dance styles.
